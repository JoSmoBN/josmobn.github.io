Story

Much like many people have done in this generation, I floated around a bit in my 20s. I went from being a musician, to working as a quality engineer, then landed as an accountant for some time. There was something during that time that was missing that I had as a working musician, the ability to create something from a skill set I had acquired.

I have always been intrigued by technology but had a very limited experience with html and css from the Myspace days. I was introduced (by my wonderful wife) to Web Development bootcamps after spending some time going back to college for Computer Science. I was already hooked with coding, but web development has allowed me to take what knowledge I had been gaining and apply it more practically.

I'm very excited about my future in this field and the field itself. The pace it is growing at is astonishing and I happy to keep growing along with it.
